Effective Date: October 27, 2023
Last Updated: October 27, 2023

Thank you for using “Job Analyzer Extension”. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our Extension.

1. What Information Do We Collect?
#

To provide the core functionality of the Extension, we process the following types of data. All data processing and storage occur locally on your device. We do not upload any of your personal data to our servers.

  1. Website Content

    • What we collect: When you browse a supported job board (e.g., LinkedIn), the Extension reads and processes text content from the page, including but not limited to the job title, company name, location, and job description.
    • Why we collect it: This is essential for the Extension’s core feature of extracting job information for skills analysis.

  2. Web History

    • What we collect: We access the URL of your currently active tab.
    • Why we collect it: To determine if the current page is a supported job board and to enable automatic analysis on Single-Page Application (SPA) websites like LinkedIn.

2. How Do We Use Your Information?
#

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Core Features: To display a summary of the job information and the results of the skills analysis in your browser’s side panel.
  • To Enhance User Experience: To ensure the Extension runs reliably and automatically on websites with dynamically loaded content.
  • Data Storage: All extracted and analyzed data is stored only in your local browser’s storage (chrome.storage.session). This data is typically cleared when you close your browser session.

3. How Do We Share Your Information?
#

In the current version, we do not share any of your data with any third parties.

All data collection, processing, and analysis are performed locally on your device.

[Disclosure for Future Feature: Notion Integration]

We plan to introduce a feature that allows you to save job analysis results to your Notion account. Only when you explicitly opt-in and authorize this feature will the following data sharing occur:

  • Data to be shared: The job analysis results you extracted (e.g., job title, company, skills list).
  • Who it is shared with: Notion’s official API.
  • Purpose of sharing: To write the data into a Notion database that you specify.
  • Authentication: To enable this, we will guide you to log in to your Notion account via the OAuth 2.0 protocol. During this process, we will obtain an authentication credential (token) for communicating with the Notion API. This token will be stored securely in your local browser. We will only access your Notion account after your authorization and will only perform data-writing operations.

Other than the case described above, we do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to any other third parties.

4. Your Choices and Rights
#

You have full control over your data. You can exercise your rights in the following ways:

  • You can clear all data stored locally by the Extension at any time through your browser’s developer tools.
  • You can uninstall the Extension from your Chrome browser at any time to stop all data processing activities.
  • For the future Notion integration, you can choose not to use it or revoke the Extension’s access at any time from your Notion account settings.

5. Children’s Privacy
#

This Extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
#

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our features or for legal reasons. We will post any changes on this page and update the “Last Updated” date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
